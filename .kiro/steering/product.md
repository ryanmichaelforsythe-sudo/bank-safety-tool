# Product

**Bank Safety Tool** — A take-home project for Nymbus.

The tool focuses on bank safety analysis: evaluating and surfacing safety-related information about banks (e.g., financial health, risk indicators, regulatory data).

## Key Principles
- Accuracy matters — this is a financial domain tool; data integrity and correctness are critical
- UI/UX should be clear and informative; users need to trust the data they see
